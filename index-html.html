Recent Publications

Evaluation of VR-based Trauma Management Training for Emergency Medicine Residents

Jacobs C, Chen S, Johnson M, et al.

Journal of Medical Education Technology, 2024

This study evaluated the effectiveness of a novel virtual reality-based trauma management simulation compared to traditional mannequin-based training. Results demonstrated significant improvements in procedural knowledge and team communication skills among residents using the VR platform.

Read Full Paper

Augmented Reality for Anatomical Education: A Systematic Review

Chen S, Jacobs C, Williams K, et al.

Anatomical Sciences Education, 2023

This systematic review examined 47 studies implementing augmented reality tools in anatomical education. The analysis revealed strong evidence for improved spatial understanding and knowledge retention when AR supplements traditional cadaveric dissection.

Read Full Paper

Haptic Feedback Systems in Virtual Surgical Training: Current State and Future Directions

Jacobs C, Peterson T, Chen S, et al.

Surgical Innovation, 2023

This paper reviews the current state of haptic feedback technology in virtual surgical training, identifying key challenges and promising approaches to creating more realistic tissue interaction models for next-generation surgical simulators.
